The 3D Slicer is developed in an ongoing collaboration between the MIT Artificial Intelligence Lab and the Surgical Planning Lab at Brigham & Women's Hospital, which is affiliated with the Harvard Medical School.

The main contributors to this joint effort are listed below.

Area Contributors
Overall concept Ron Kikinis, Ferenc Jolesz, Eric Grimson, William Wells III
Major designer and implementer Dave Gering (1997-1999), Lauren O'Donnell (1999-)
Prototype Noby Hata (1997), Ron Kikinis
OpenMR interface Arya Nabavi (1998-1999), Ferenc Jolesz
Measurement tools William Lorensen, Peter Everett
Robot simulation tools Noby Hata, Oliver Schorr

MI registration William Wells III
Virtual endoscopy tool Delphine Nain
