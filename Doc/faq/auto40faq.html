• What about Linux?

    Slicer works fine under Linux, and as of Slicer version 2 we are providing a binary distribution for Red Hat Linux 7.3. You can get a distribution at https://share.spl.harvard.edu/share/birn/public/software/slicer2-2002-11-linux-x86.tar.gz
    More information on building the source code can be found here.
