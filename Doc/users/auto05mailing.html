There are two mailing lists, one for users (slicer-users@bwh.harvard.edu) and one for developers (slicer-devel@bwh.harvard.edu).
To subscribe to the users' mailing list, send email to slicer-users-request@bwh.harvard.edu with the word subscribe as the subject.
To subscribe to the developers' mailing list, send email to slicer-devel-request@bwh.harvard.edu with the word subscribe as the subject. To subscribe to the user mailing list, send mail to slicer-users-request@bwh.harvard.edu with the word subscribe as the subject.

The web interface for the mailing lists is at https://www.spl.harvard.edu/mailman/listinfo/slicer-users.
